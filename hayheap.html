The Potential

Hayheap is more than a search tool - it's the foundation for a living knowledge graph of research, people, and markets.

  • Shifting recruitment models: As AI tools increasingly help candidates pass technical screens, hiring will rely less on rote skill tests and more on understanding genuine expertise, curiosity, and emerging research contributions.
  • Future use-cases we can't yet see: A comprehensive research-to-people map enables possibilities we haven't imagined - from forecasting under- or over-funded domains, to spotting revolutions versus incremental improvements.
  • Radar for science & markets: Correctly mapped, this system becomes a "radar" for:
    • Where talent pools are emerging
    • Which research areas are poised for commercialization
    • Which fields are at risk of hype vs. genuine breakthroughs (think the superconductivity craze)
  • Beyond today's horizon: By anchoring insights in a durable knowledge base, Hayheap can support not just recruiting and venture scouting, but also science policy, national R&D strategy, and entirely new market categories.
